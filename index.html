About Us

At Abyssinia House, we bring the soul of Ethiopia to the rhythm of modern life. Rooted in the warmth of traditional coffee ceremonies and the vibrancy of local customs, our café is where heritage meets innovation. Whether you're sipping a rich buna brewed with reverence or enjoying a refreshing juice made from indigenous fruits, every experience is a tribute to Ethiopia’s flavors—reimagined for today.
We celebrate cultural authenticity with a fresh perspective—think classic woven décor alongside sleek finishes, and age-old hospitality served in eco-friendly to-go cups. Our space is designed to be as flexible as it is inviting, perfect for those who want to linger or simply grab and go.
More than just a coffee and juice house, Abyssinia House is a daily ritual, a cultural experience, and a modern convenience—wrapped in sustainability and served with pride.
